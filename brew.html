Brew Guide

French Press

Use 18g coffee to 300ml water, steep for 4 minutes. Press and pour.

Pour Over

Use 20g coffee to 320ml water, pour slowly in circular motion for best results.

Aeropress

Use 16g coffee with inverted method, brew for 2 minutes before pressing.
